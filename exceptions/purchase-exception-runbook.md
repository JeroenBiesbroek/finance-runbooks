# Purchase Exception Runbook

**Owner:** Finance Lead

## When to use
When purchase invoices fail to process, approval is overdue, creditor discrepancies are detected, or duplicate invoices are found.

## Common exceptions

### P-01: Missing creditor
1. Verify supplier is legitimate
2. Create creditor in Exact Online
3. Set up payment terms and default accounts
4. Process the invoice

### P-02: Amount discrepancy
1. Compare invoice to purchase order / agreement
2. Contact supplier if needed
3. Supplier error: request corrected invoice
4. Acceptable difference: process with Finance Lead approval

### P-03: Duplicate purchase invoice
1. Verify by comparing number, supplier, amount, date
2. Delete before posting or reverse after posting
3. Review process for improvement

### P-04: Approval overdue (> 5 business days)
1. Identify approver, send reminder
2. If unavailable: escalate to Finance Lead for delegation

### P-05: Incorrect GL account
1. Identify correct account
2. Create correcting journal (Finance Lead approval)
3. Update relevant configuration

## Escalation
- Past-due invoices → Finance Lead
- Supplier disputes → Finance Lead
- Suspected fraud → Finance Lead + CFO
