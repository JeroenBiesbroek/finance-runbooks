### Exception Types
- `data-error` — incorrect data requiring correction
- `missing-evidence` — evidence not yet obtained
- `unexplained-variance` — material variance without explanation
- `unresolved-reconciliation` — reconciliation difference not cleared
- `process-gap` — runbook or procedure gap discovered
- `timing-difference` — expected to resolve in next period
- `pending-external` — waiting for external party (bank, supplier, customer)
